Ugali

Ugali is a nice food found in Tanzania. Most Tanzanian use it as their main food. It is nice when eaten with beef and more interesting when eaten with Sardines.

Rice

Rice is also among the main food in Tanzanian Community. It is made from paddy rice commonly known as Mpunga in Swahili Language. It is commonly found in region with wet lands and cold araes like Mbeya, Tabora and Morogogoro and Shinyanga. It is the most sweet food for many People in Tanzania.

Vegetables

This are green plants that are commonly used as food for humans. Most of them carry nutrients rich in vitamins and minerals. Their main role is to provide vitamin which add on protection cells' strength Example of Vegetables found in Tanzania are; Matembele, Mchicha, Spinach, Sukuma wiki, Mnafu, and Kabichi.
